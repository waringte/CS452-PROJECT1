Controls:
  Jump with spacebar
  change your color with:
    a - red
    s - green
    d - blue
    
How to Play:
  Your character(cube) changes colors and moves along the platforms. You can't stay on a platform that's the same color
  as you. You will begin moving towards walls of various colors. You can move through walls the same color as you but
  if you hit a wall that's a different color you lose the game. If you can last for 3 minutes, you win.
  Your score is output to the console.
